No — that's exactly the problem we're solving. BhoomiFi was designed for farmers who have never had a credit card or formal loan. Your Harvest Score is built entirely from your agricultural data: crop type, land size, location, and farming conditions. No CIBIL score required, ever.

Your score is generated by a machine learning model trained on real agricultural yield data from across India. It considers crop viability in your region, land size, estimated yield potential, and risk factors like seasonal patterns. The AI then explains every single factor — so you always know why you got the score you did. No hidden logic.

For the initial Harvest Score, you only need to provide your crop type, land size in acres, and your village or district location. No documents are required at this stage. If a lender decides to proceed with a loan offer, they may request land records (7/12 extract) separately.

Your data is used only to generate your Harvest Score and match you to relevant loan offers and schemes. We do not sell your data to third parties. Lenders only see your score and profile after you choose to share it — they cannot access your raw farm data without your consent.

Once your Harvest Score is generated, BhoomiFi shares your credit profile (score, crop, location, land size) with registered lenders in our network. Lenders then submit loan offers — interest rate, maximum loan amount, tenure. You see all offers side by side and choose the one that works for you. You are never locked into a single lender.

BhoomiFi currently matches farmers to PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi (direct income support), Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana or PMFBY (crop insurance), Kisan Credit Card or KCC (revolving credit for farm inputs), and the Soil Health Card scheme. More schemes are added regularly. Eligibility is determined automatically based on your farm profile.

Yes — completely free. Farmers never pay to get a Harvest Score, view loan offers, or discover government schemes. BhoomiFi earns from lenders who pay a small fee when a farmer successfully accepts a loan offer. Your access is always free, always will be.
